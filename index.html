Privacy Policy for Panda SAT Prep

Last updated: July 18, 2026

Panda SAT Prep does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

What the app does

Panda SAT Prep is an SAT practice app. All of your data — your progress, tokens, XP, streaks, flagged questions, and answer history — is stored only on your own device using Apple's standard local storage. This data never leaves your device. We do not have servers, and we do not receive, see, or have access to any of it.

Notifications

If you enable streak reminders, Panda SAT Prep uses Apple's local notification system to remind you to practice. These reminders are scheduled entirely on your device. No notification data is sent to us or to any third party.

No third parties

Panda SAT Prep does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with anyone, because no data ever leaves your device.

No account required

There is no sign-up, login, or account system. Nothing you enter is tied to your identity.

Children's privacy

Because the app collects no data of any kind, it does not collect personal information from children or anyone else.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes — for example, if a future version of the app adds an online feature — we will update this page and the "Last updated" date above before that feature ships.
